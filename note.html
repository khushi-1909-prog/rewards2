Note about the upcoming e-commerce platform

Better Buy is a shopping platform that offers different kinds of shopping products and services. You can buy different types of products like clothing, footwear, electronics, clothing, furniture, and cosmetics.

The interface you will use represents a pilot version and has been deliberately simplified, including its design, colour scheme, available functions, and the way products are displayed. For example, clothing items are presented in a single general category without further subdivisions.

For this study, we are only considering clothing items.

After reading the information above, you are required to browse the Better Buy platform and select any product and place the order.
