Note about the upcoming e-commerce platform

Zyntho is an upcoming shopping platform that offers different kinds of shopping products. You can buy a variety of products, including clothing, footwear, electronics, furniture, and cosmetics.

The interface you will use represents a pilot version and has been deliberately simplified, including its design, colour scheme, available functions, and the way products are displayed. The platform seeks your view related to interface before it formally launches. In this interface, items are presented in a single general category without further subdivisions.

To test the interface, imagine yourself shopping for the footwear category.

Now you can browse the Zyntho platform, select, and place an order for any product in the footwear category.
